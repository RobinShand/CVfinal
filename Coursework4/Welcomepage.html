Welcome to my Website

This is my website I've been working on for the past while, you can see that I've tried to make it as accessible as possible with the basic design, no scrolling or moving parts and alt-text on every image.

I've also decided to style the <p> tag by changing the font to an easier the read font 'Verdana' as well as increasing the font size to 16px so that people who stuggle to read small text will hopefully have an easier time reading small bits of information on the page.
